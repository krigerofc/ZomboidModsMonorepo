title=Zombie Disguise
description=Cover yourself in zombie blood and entrails to blend in with the horde. A high-risk, high-reward disguise mechanic — the bacteria will slowly make you sick.
description=
description=🧟 How It Works
description=
description=1. Kill a zombie and find its body on the ground
description=2. Right-click the corpse → select "Disguise"
description=3. Your character spends ~7 in-game minutes applying blood and entrails to their body
description=4. Zombies stop detecting you as a threat — you move freely through the horde
description=
description=☣️ Bacterial Contamination — Time is Against You
description=
description=The disguise doesn't last forever. Zombie entrails carry bacteria that progressively make you sick:
description=
description=Time with Disguise Effect
description=0 – 3h in-game Safe window — no negative effects
description=3h – 4h Mild: nausea + unhappiness
description=4h – 5h Moderate: nausea + stress
description=5h – 6h Severe: nausea + stress + panic
description=6h+ Critical: full illness — nausea, fatigue, panic (debilitating but not lethal)
description=
description=🚿 Removing the Disguise
description=
description=The disguise is removed gradually — one body part at a time — as you wash yourself:
description=
description=Shower or sink → washes parts you clean
description=Rain → slowly washes blood off exposed parts
description=Full wash → removes disguise and stops contamination completely
description=Zombie aggression returns as each disguised part is washed off
description=
description=⚠️ When the Disguise is Disabled (tooltip explains)
description=
description=Already disguised → "You are already disguised."
description=Action interrupted (moved or took damage) → disguise not applied
description=
description=📝 Notes
description=
description=Multiplayer compatible — server-authoritative state, no exploits
description=Single Player and split-screen co-op supported
description=No extra items required — just a zombie corpse
description=Blood is only tracked for the disguise mechanic — combat blood is unaffected
