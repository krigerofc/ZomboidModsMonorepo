title=Basic Vehicle Maintenance
description=A simple quality-of-life mod that adds casual "duct-tape-and-pray" repair options for vehicle parts. Not a full mechanics overhaul — just a quick workaround for when your car is falling apart and you need to keep moving. Made in a playful spirit: tape it up and everything's fine.
description=
description=🛠️ Features
description=
description=Right-click any part in the mechanics screen → "Casual Repair" option appears
description=Works on all vehicles (vans, trucks, police cars, modded vehicles)
description=Uses common scrap materials found in the world
description=Mechanics skill reduces failure chance (starts at level 2, -2% per level, min 5%)
description=Multiplayer compatible (server-authoritative validation)
description=⚠️ Repair Cap — You Can't Fix It Back to Perfect
description=
description=Each part has a max condition cap. Casual repair adds a fixed gain, clamped at the cap.
description=

description=🎲 Random Events
description=
description=Every repair can trigger:
description=
description=🤕 Hand injury (5%) — scratches hand, character says "Ouch, cut my hand!"
description=🔥 Burn (3%) — only on heat parts: Engine, Muffler, Gas Tank
description=🔧 Tool breaks (5%) — removes a tool from inventory
description=📦 Bonus material (10%) — server grants extra material
description=⭐ Critical success (5%) — extra condition gain
description=😓 Stress (10%) — applies stress to the character
description=🚫 When the Option is Disabled (tooltip explains)
description=
description=Part at 100% → "Part is undamaged. Nothing to repair."
description=Above the cap → "Part is too well-maintained for casual repair."
description=Not installed → "Part is not installed on the vehicle."
description=Missing items → shows exactly what you're missing
